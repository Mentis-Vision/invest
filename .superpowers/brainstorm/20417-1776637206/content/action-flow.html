How chips flow into the journal

Today we have two disconnected action systems — the Strategy "Done/Snooze/ Dismiss" chips, and the History action tracker. You're right: acting on the hero should create a journal entry. Here's the proposed unified flow.

1. Strategy Next Move hero (on Dashboard, integrated)
⚡ Next move · today · HIGH
↘ Reduce LINK to 25% of portfolio
Concentration at 51% creates material single-position risk.
2a. "I did some" modal (partial)

We recommended reducing LINK to 25%.

How much did you actually do?
Why that amount? (optional)
2b. "Dismiss" modal (ignored)

Skip today's recommendation.

Why? (helps your pattern insights)
3. Shows up in History · "Your actions"
LINK SELL (trim) ~ Partial Reduced to 36% (trimmed 15% not the full 25%) today
"Didn't want to fully exit, still bullish on the thesis"

One decision to make: how much do we nudge the user to leave a note?

A

Required on Partial + Dismiss

Can't save until the note is filled. Builds a richer journal and stronger pattern insights. But feels like friction — some users won't bother and will just click "Done" to skip the modal.

B

Optional, but we ask every time

Modal always opens with the text field; "Save" works with it blank. Most users will skip; a few will write great notes. My recommendation — soft accountability without friction.

C

Skip the modal entirely

Click "Partial" or "Dismiss" → row just appears in History with no detail. They can add a note later by expanding the row. Fastest; leanest on the pattern insights card since most rows will be noteless.
