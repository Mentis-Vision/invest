Can we trust the counterfactual?
Math: we already store every ticker's close price daily (ticker_market_daily). The three bars compare portfolio value today under three scenarios, using real historical closes.

What it DOES handle: price changes, shares traded, cash from sells.
What it DOESN'T: taxes, broker fees, where you redeployed the proceeds (if you trimmed LINK and bought SPY, "actual path" will miss the SPY gain).
Fidelity tag: every card carries "Directional only — ignores taxes, fees, and redeployment" so users don't mistake it for a brokerage P&L statement.
