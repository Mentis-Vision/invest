Decision-support first. User lands on a single recommended action — rebalance, trim a winner, research a flagged holding — with evidence. They do it or dismiss it; done in 90 seconds.

Research-first. User comes with a ticker or thesis on their mind. The app pulls data, applies three lenses, and returns verdicts they cross-reference against their own.

Journal-first. User lands on "here's what you said, here's what happened" — a feedback loop that sharpens their judgment over time. Research is in service of the journal.
