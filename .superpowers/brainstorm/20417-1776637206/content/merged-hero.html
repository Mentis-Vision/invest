Where does the counterfactual live?

A

Compact on Dashboard, full on Journal

On the Dashboard, AFTER you've acted on a Next Move, a small strip appears: "You trimmed LINK 4 days ago → +$180 vs counterfactual. See details →" Full three-bar comparison lives on the Journal.

Pros: Dashboard gets a little reward, not overwhelming.
Cons: Two places to maintain the component.

B

Journal only — clean separation

Dashboard stays "today-focused." Full three-bar counterfactual only on the Journal page. You go there when you want to reflect (weekly, not daily).

Pros: Single source of truth. Dashboard never feels retrospective.
Cons: Might forget it exists; less dopamine for the "I acted" moment.

C

Full bar chart on both Dashboard AND Journal

Same three-bar widget everywhere. Dashboard shows it under the hero when an action has been logged. Journal shows it as the primary card.

Pros: Strongest reinforcement loop.
Cons: Dashboard density keeps climbing.

My recommendation: A. You get the reward on Dashboard ("my action is paying off"), but the deeper analysis is in the Journal where it belongs. Dashboard stays forward-looking ("what's next"); Journal is backward-looking ("what did I do"). Splitting the two keeps each coherent.
